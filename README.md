# Chinese WebNovel Master

> Don't just write novels. Engineer reader addiction.

The first AI Skill designed specifically for Chinese web-fiction success.

Unlike traditional writing assistants, Chinese WebNovel Master focuses on what really matters:

**Reader Retention.**

## Features

### 📈 Market-Oriented Story Planning

Analyze:

- Genre trends
- Reader demand
- Competition level
- Platform suitability

Supported platforms:

- Tomato Novel (番茄小说)
- Qidian (起点中文网)
- Feilu (飞卢小说网)
- Jinjiang (晋江文学城)
- Qimao (七猫小说)

---

### 🚀 Viral Story Generator

Generate:

- Story concepts
- Unique selling points
- High-conversion plot ideas
- Commercially viable story directions

### 🧩 Plot Consistency Engine

Track:

Analyze every chapter:

- Suspense density

- Reader continuation probability

Produce more natural web-fiction writing.
