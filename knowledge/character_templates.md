# Character Templates

## Protagonist Template

Purpose:
Drive the story forward.

Elements:

* Core Desire
* External Goal
* Internal Need
* Fatal Flaw
* Hidden Strength
* Growth Arc

Questions:

* What does the protagonist want?
* Why can't they get it?
* What must they become to succeed?

---

## Antagonist Template

Purpose:
Create meaningful opposition.

Elements:

* Goal
* Resources
* Power Base
* Motivation
* Weakness

Rules:

* Antagonists believe they are right.
* Antagonists must have understandable motives.

---

## Mentor Template

Purpose:
Guide the protagonist.

Elements:

* Knowledge
* Secret
* Limitation
* Teaching Style

Common Functions:

* Introduce the world
* Explain power systems
* Trigger growth

---

## Rival Template

Purpose:
Create competition.

Elements:

* Similar Goal
* Different Method
* Constant Comparison

Benefits:

* Motivates protagonist
* Creates tension
* Shows growth

---

## Love Interest Template

Purpose:
Create emotional engagement.

Elements:

* Attraction
* Conflict
* Compatibility
* Emotional Stakes

Rules:

* Romance should affect the plot.
* Relationship must evolve.

---

## Supporting Character Template

Purpose:
Strengthen the main cast.

Functions:

* Ally
* Comic Relief
* Informant
* Specialist
* Emotional Support

Rule:

Every supporting character should serve at least one story function.
