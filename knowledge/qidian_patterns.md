# Qidian Novel Patterns

## Audience

Primary Audience:

* Male 18-40
* Long-form readers
* Progression fantasy fans

---

## Core Characteristics

### World Building

Readers expect:

* Deep lore
* Consistent rules
* Historical background
* Exploration opportunities

Rule:

The world should feel larger than the story.

---

### Power Progression

Readers want:

* Visible growth
* Meaningful breakthroughs
* Clear ranking systems

Rule:

Every advancement should matter.

---

### Long-Term Plot

Structure:

* Early Arc
* Mid Arc
* Expansion Arc
* Endgame Arc

Target Length:

* 1,000+ chapters
* Multiple major storylines

---

### Slow Burn Rewards

Characteristics:

* Delayed gratification
* Large payoffs
* Long preparation

Examples:

* Revenge
* Realm breakthrough
* Kingdom building

---

## Reader Retention Factors

Strong:

* Progress
* Mystery
* Exploration
* Competition
* Discovery

Weak:

* Repetition
* Excessive exposition
* Meaningless filler

---

## Success Checklist

Before publishing:

* World rules defined
* Progression path defined
* Major factions defined
* Long-term goal defined
* Future arcs outlined

Rule:

A Qidian novel is a marathon, not a sprint.
