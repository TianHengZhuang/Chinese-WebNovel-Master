# Suspense Hook Library

Version: 1.0

Purpose:

Maximize reader continuation rate.

A hook is any unresolved element that creates psychological tension.

Rule:

Every chapter should end with at least one hook.

Never end a chapter in a fully resolved state.

---

# Hook Categories

1. Question Hook

2. Threat Hook

3. Discovery Hook

4. Identity Hook

5. Countdown Hook

6. Reward Hook

7. Relationship Hook

8. Secret Hook

9. Foreshadow Hook

10. Reversal Hook

---

# Question Hook

Create unanswered questions.

Examples:

那封信里到底写了什么？

为什么所有人都在隐瞒这件事？

那座废弃工厂究竟发生过什么？

Reader Emotion:

Curiosity

---

# Threat Hook

Introduce danger.

Examples:

三天后，怪物将进入城市。

如果失败，他将失去一切。

系统提示：

死亡倒计时开始。

Reader Emotion:

Fear

Urgency

---

# Discovery Hook

Reveal unexpected information.

Examples:

保险柜里竟然是她的照片。

他发现了隐藏名单。

地图上出现了新的坐标。

Reader Emotion:

Curiosity

Anticipation

---

# Identity Hook

Reveal hidden identity.

Examples:

原来校花就是幕后老板。

那个乞丐竟然是首富。

师父真实身份曝光。

Reader Emotion:

Surprise

Status Reversal

---

# Countdown Hook

Create time pressure.

Examples:

距离考试还有7天。

距离灾难降临还有24小时。

距离系统任务结束还有30分钟。

Reader Emotion:

Urgency

---

# Reward Hook

Promise future gain.

Examples:

奖励将在明天发放。

宝藏即将开启。

隐藏功能即将解锁。

Reader Emotion:

Expectation

---

# Secret Hook

Introduce hidden information.

Examples:

地下室还有第二层。

真正的任务并未公布。

还有一个人知道真相。

Reader Emotion:

Mystery

---

# Reversal Hook

Sudden change.

Examples:

任务奖励竟然变成惩罚。

胜利后却收到死亡通知。

系统突然失效。

Reader Emotion:

Shock

---

# Chapter Ending Formula

Strong Ending:

Question

*

Threat

*

Discovery

Example:

他打开保险柜。

里面竟然是一张自己的死亡证明。

与此同时。

系统提示：

死亡倒计时开始。

---

# Face Slap Hook

Humiliation

↓

Challenge

↓

Evidence

↓

Reversal

↓

Recognition

↓

New Threat

Never stop after victory.

Always introduce a larger challenge.

---

# Curiosity Gap Rule

Never answer all questions.

For every answer:

Create a new question.

Question A

↓

Answer A

*

Question B

↓

Answer B

*

Question C

---

# Hook Density

Target:

One major hook every chapter.

One medium hook every scene.

One micro hook every 500-800 words.

---

# Weak Endings

Avoid:

睡觉

吃饭

回家

普通聊天

无意义总结

情绪平稳结束

These reduce continuation rate.

---

# Hook Quality Checklist

Before publishing:

□ Unanswered question exists

□ Future event implied

□ Emotional tension remains

□ Reader wants next chapter

□ Curiosity level high

□ Conflict unresolved

If any item is missing:

Rewrite ending.

---

# Final Principle

Readers continue reading for unresolved tension.

The moment all tension disappears,

retention collapses.
